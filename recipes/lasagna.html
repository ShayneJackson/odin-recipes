Miracle Lasagna

Description

This is the simplist and most delicious lasagna you can make!

Ingredients
  • 1 (26 oz) jar Prego sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. Spread 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch). Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce. Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and pasta sauce. Cover.
  2. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour. Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes.
